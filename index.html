I'M A PRODUCT DESIGNER

Welcome to my digital corner! I'm passionate about creating meaningful experiences at the intersection of technology, design, and human needs. My journey in software engineering and product management has taught me that the best solutions come from deeply understanding the people we're designing for.

Currently pursuing my Masters in Engineering Design, I focus on bringing together strategy, UX research, and technical implementation. Whether I'm conducting user interviews, wireframing interfaces, or writing code, I approach every challenge with curiosity and empathy.

Beyond the screen, you'll find me experimenting in the kitchen with new baking recipes, tending to my garden, or playing piano. These creative outlets fuel my design thinking and remind me that the best innovations often come from unexpected places.

I believe in the power of collaborative design and authentic storytelling. Every product has a story to tell, and every user has a story worth hearing. My goal is to bridge these narratives, creating experiences that resonate on a human level.
